Vastu Shastra is the traditional Indian science of directional energy and spatial planning. Where interior design focuses on how a space looks, Vastu looks at how energy, light, airflow and the five elements move through it — and how that movement affects the people living or working inside.

A site visit with a compass reading is the most reliable method for land. If that isn't possible right away, a layout plan, the plot's boundary directions and photographs from each corner let us do a preliminary review before an on-site geopathic stress mapping.

For remote consultations, we work from your floor plan, photographs and a few details about the occupants and their concerns. Radionics-based remote analysis is then used to identify imbalances, and corrections are shared as a written action plan.
